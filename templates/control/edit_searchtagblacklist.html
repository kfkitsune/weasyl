Searchtag Blacklist Information

Tags you add here will not be able to be added to your submitted items by other users. You may still add these tags to your submissions. One wildcard per entry is permitted, in any position, and will cause any tag which matches the omitted characters to be blocked; entries with a wildcard must have at least two alphanumeric characters. Examples of valid blacklist tags are as follows:

  • mu* - Blocks murmurs, muzzle, or similar.
  • p*e - Blocks peregrine, plumage, or similar.
  • *us - Blocks superstitious, outrageous, or similar.
  • Standard tags may also be used, such as: red_fox or hermaphrodite.
