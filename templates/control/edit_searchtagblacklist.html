Searchtag Blacklist Information

Tags you add here will not be able to be added to your submitted items by other users. You may, however, still add these tags to your own submissions. In addition to blocking individual tags, you can forbid tag patterns by using * as a wildcard. Wildcard entries must have at least two additional characters. For example, sun* matches sunrise and sunset; *_pen matches fountain_pen and ballpoint_pen.
