Global Searchtag Blacklist Information

Search tags added here will not be able to be added to the list of search tags for an item, except by the owner of the item in question. One wildcard per entry is permitted, in any position, and will cause any tag which matches the omitted characters to be blocked; entries with a wildcard must have at least two alphanumeric characters. Examples of valid blacklist tags are as follows:

  • mu* - Blocks murmur, muzzle, or similar.
  • p*e - Blocks peregrine, plumage, or similar.
  • *us - Blocks superstitious, outrageous, or similar.
  • Normal tags may also be used, such as: red_fox or hermaphrodite
